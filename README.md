## 简体中文

### 🚀 项目简介
随着沉浸式娱乐（剧本杀、密室逃脱、卡牌桌游）市场的爆发式增长，游戏剧本的质量成为核心竞争力。然而，当前市场面临剧本逻辑漏洞多、数值平衡差、玩家体验不均等痛点。“衡逻辑”致力于利用数学工具为开发者提供专业的“逻辑质检标准”。

### 🛠 核心技术
1. **数值平衡模拟 (Monte Carlo)**: 通过 Python 编写脚本进行 $10^6$ 次对局模拟，确保胜率偏差 $\sigma < 5\%$。
2. **状态转移分析 (Markov Chain)**: 预测玩家在不同阶段的停留时长，识别“冗长乏味点”。
3. **逻辑闭环检测 (Graph Theory)**: 构建剧情线索有向图，利用广度优先搜索 (BFS) 检测逻辑死路。

### 📦 产品与服务
- **逻辑审计报告**: 提供角色强度的正态分布曲线及修正方案。
- **逻辑拓扑图**: 可视化展示剧情走向，标注潜在漏洞。
- **“衡逻辑”质量认证**: 为通过审计的项目颁发认证标识，提升产品溢价。

### 👥 团队优势
团队由数学系本科生组成，具备扎实的《概率论与数理统计》、《组合数学》及《图论》基础，能够将复杂的商业问题快速转化为数学语言。

## English Version

### 🚀 Introduction
With the explosive growth of the immersive entertainment market (Script Kill/Jubensha, Escape Rooms, Card Games), game quality has become the core competitiveness. "EquiliLogic" provides professional numerical balancing and logic auditing services using mathematical modeling.

### 🛠 Core Technology
- **Numerical Balance**: Monte Carlo simulations ($N=10^6$) to ensure win-rate deviation $\sigma < 5\%$.
- **Process Optimization**: Markov Chain state transition analysis to identify pacing issues.
- **Logic Auditing**: Directed graph modeling ($G = (V, E)$) and BFS algorithms to detect logic dead-ends.

### 📦 Products & Services
1. **Logic Audit Report**: Provides strength distribution curves and adjustment suggestions.
2. **Logic Topology Map**: Visualizes plot branches and marks potential logic holes.
3. **Certification**: "EquiliLogic" quality seal to enhance product value.

### 👥 Team Advantage
Our team consists of Mathematics undergraduates with strong backgrounds in Probability, Statistics, and Graph Theory, specialized in translating complex business logic into precise mathematical models.
